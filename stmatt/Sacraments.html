Sacraments

Baptism

In the Sacrament of Baptism, we are received into the Church.

First Reconciliation

Reconciliation is the sacrament in which God's loving mercy is poured out upon an individual.

First Communion

In our First Communion, we receive Jesus Body, Blood, Soul and Divinity. He is given to us in the form of ordinary bread and wine, which has been consecrated by the priest.

Confirmation

In Confirmation, we are given the gift of the Holy Spirit.

Matrimony

The covenant of marriage is celebrated in Matrimony. A priest blesses the couple as they exchange vows of love and honor in the presence of family, friends, and God.
