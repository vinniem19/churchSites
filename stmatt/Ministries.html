Ministries

Music

Music is the heart of the liturgy. It lifts the soul.

Altar Servers

Altar servers play an integral role during the Mass; they help support the priest during throughout the liturgy.

Lectors

Lectors have the priviledge of proclaiming the Word of God or scripture passages to the congregation.

Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion

Ministers of Holy Communion distribute the consecrated body and blood of Christ to the people during communion.

Ministers to the Sick

Ministers to the sick visit with those who cannot attend the weekend Mass because of sickness. Some people may be in hospitals, nursing homes, or even their own homes. They pray with and distribute Holy Communion to those who are unable to receive Jesus in regular attendance at Mass.
